Privacy Policy for Baby Bump

Last updated: January 2025

Information We Collect

Baby Bump is designed with your privacy in mind. We collect and process the following information:

  • Photos: Photos you take or select are stored locally on your device only
  • Album Data: Album names, descriptions, and organization data stored locally
  • App Usage: Basic analytics to improve app performance (optional)

How We Use Your Information

  • To provide the core functionality of organizing your pregnancy photos
  • To improve app performance and fix bugs
  • To provide customer support when requested

Data Storage

All your photos and personal data are stored locally on your device. We do not upload your photos to our servers or any cloud service without your explicit consent.

Third-Party Services

We may use the following third-party services:

  • Analytics: To understand app usage and improve performance
  • Crash Reporting: To identify and fix app crashes
  • Payment Processing: For premium subscriptions (handled securely by Apple/Google)

Permissions

The app requests the following permissions:

  • Camera: To take new photos for your albums
  • Photo Library: To select existing photos from your device
  • Storage: To save and organize your photos locally

Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or share your personal information with third parties, except:

  • When required by law
  • To protect our rights and safety
  • With your explicit consent

Children's Privacy

Our app is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Delete your personal data
  • Export your data
  • Opt out of analytics

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new privacy policy on this page.
