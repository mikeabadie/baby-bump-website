Privacy Policy for Baby Bump

Last updated: October 30, 2025

Information We Collect

Baby Bump is designed with your privacy in mind. We collect and process the following information:

  • Photos: Photos you take or select are stored locally on your device only
  • Album Data: Album names, descriptions, and organization data stored locally
  • App Usage: Basic analytics to improve app performance (optional)

How We Use Your Information

  • To provide the core functionality of organizing your pregnancy photos
  • To improve app performance and fix bugs
  • To provide customer support when requested

Data Storage

All your photos and personal data are stored locally on your device. We do not upload your photos to our servers or any cloud service without your explicit consent.

Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • Sentry: For crash reporting and error monitoring to improve app stability
  • RevenueCat: For managing in-app subscriptions and purchases
  • Apple App Store: For payment processing and subscription management (handled securely by Apple)

These services may collect limited technical information (crash reports, device type, app version) to help us improve the app. No photos or personal album data is shared with these services.

Permissions

The app requests the following permissions:

  • Camera: To take new photos for your albums
  • Photo Library: To select existing photos from your device
  • Storage: To save and organize your photos locally

Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or share your personal information with third parties, except:

  • When required by law
  • To protect our rights and safety
  • With your explicit consent

Children's Privacy

Our app is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your data: All your photos and albums are stored locally on your device and accessible at any time
  • Delete your data: You can delete individual albums, photos, or all data through the app's Settings > Data & Privacy section
  • Export your data: Your photos remain in your device's photo library and can be backed up through iCloud or iTunes
  • Data portability: Since all data is stored locally, you maintain full control and can transfer your device backup
